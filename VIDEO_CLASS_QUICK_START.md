## How to Start a Video Class

### Step 1: Create a Course
1. Go to "My Courses" section
2. Click "Add Course +"
3. Fill in:
   - **Course Code**: e.g., `UI101`
   - **Course Name**: e.g., `UI Design Fundamentals`
   - **Schedule**: e.g., `MWF 09:00-10:00`
   - **Number of Students**: e.g., `20`
   - **Session Date**: Pick a date
   - **Session Time**: Pick a time

### Step 2: Generate Meeting Link
1. Click **"Generate Meeting Link"** button
2. A unique Meeting ID will be created
3. Copy the ID: e.g., `APKL-ABC123-DEF456`
4. Share this ID with your students (via email/announcement)

### Step 3: Start the Class
1. Click **"Start Video Class"** button
2. **Allow camera and microphone access** when your browser asks
3. The Zoom-like interface will open
4. Your video feed appears in the center

## Common Questions

**Q: Can students see each other?**
- A: Not yet. Currently, students see teacher and screen share. Multi-student video coming soon.

**Q: Is the class recorded?**
- A: Not automatically. Recording feature coming in next update.

**Q: How long can a class last?**
- A: As long as you want! No time limits.

**Q: Can I host multiple classes simultaneously?**
- A: Create separate meetings for each class.

**Q: Do students need special software?**
- A: No! Browser-based. Just open student portal and enter Meeting ID.

**Q: What if my internet drops?**
- A: Class ends. Students see "Connection lost". You can restart and share new Meeting ID.
